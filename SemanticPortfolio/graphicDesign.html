Counterfeit Poster

This assignment for Text (ATLS 2300) was to work on using graphic design skills to change a public piece of printed media to subvert the original message. I chose this target because I am not a fan of using AI to do skills that you should know how to do. The message of this flyer is geared towards those who want to build their business using AI rather than doing it themselves. I want to modify it to make it sound like using AI is a lazy-man's way of getting these skills and building your business. Along with the text, I will change the people’s photos and names to seem to be AI-generated when you take a closer look at it. It has enough text I think it will give me the room to play around with the wording to make it subversive. Many people in the CTD program share the same feelings towards AI, and I think using it as the focus of subversion will help it to be funny. The phrasing of “Limited spots available!” and the claim of “how to go from zero-to-one, fast!” also makes it seem a bit like a scam to be offering all these things with their program.
