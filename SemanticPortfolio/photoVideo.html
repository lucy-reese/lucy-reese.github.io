The Wizard of Oz (Retold by Kids)

This video was made for my Image course (ATLS 2100) in which the videography unit project prompt was to retell a children's story. I had my younger siblings retell the story of "The Wizard of Oz" and I lip synced as well as acted out the story based on their recounted tale.
